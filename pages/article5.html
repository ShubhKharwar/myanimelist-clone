'Isshun de Chiryou shiteita noni Yakutatazu to Tsuihou sareta Tensai Chiyushi, Yami Healer toshite Tanoshiku Ikiru' Reveals Main Cast, Production Staff, Teaser Promo for Spring 2025

by DatRandomDude

Nov 8, 11:59 AM | 19 Comments

The official website for the television anime adaptation of Sakaku Hishikawa's Isshun de Chiryou shiteita noni Yakutatazu to Tsuihou sareta Tensai Chiyushi, Yami Healer toshite Tanoshiku Ikiru (The Brilliant Healer's New Life in the Shadows) light novel revealed the main cast, production staff, teaser visual (pictured), and a teaser promotional video on Friday. The anime is scheduled to premiere April 2025.

Cast
Zenos: Shougo Sakata (Chiyu Mahou no Machigatta Tsukaikata)
Lily: Miharu Hanai (Isekai Yururi Kikou: Kosodateshinagara Boukensha Shimasu)
Carmilla: Youko Hikasa (High School DxD)

Staff
Director: ou Yoshizaki (Chiikawa episode director)
Script: Daishou Miyagi
Character Design: Denpuougi, Yoshihiro Sawada
Art Director: Hiroshi Gouroku (Isekai Shoukan wa Nidome desu)
Color Design: Moe Kawada
Director of Photography: Kouhei Tanada (Arknights: Reimei Zensou)
Sound Director: Hiroto Morishita (Tsue to Tsurugi no Wistoria)
Music: Harumi Fuuki (Shinmai Renkinjutsushi no Tenpo Keiei)
Studio: Makaria

Hishikawa began writing the adventure fantasy series, also known by its short title Yami Healer, on the Shousetsuka ni Narou website in November 2020. SB Creative began printing the novel under its GA Novel label the following October, featuring illustrations by Daburyuu. The sixth volume shipped on March 15, with the seventh volume going on sale on November 15. Yami Healer has a cumulative 200,000 copies of its volumes in print.

A manga adaptation by Ten Junoichi launched on the GA Comic website in January 2022. The third volume went on sale on March 15, with the fourth volume also planned for a November 15 release.
